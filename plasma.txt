== Flatpak Theme Fixes ==

Settings > Workspace > Startup and Shutdown > Autostart > Add program > xsettingsd
cd ~/.themes/
ln -s Breeze-dark-gtk Breeze-Dark # Required if the GTK theme has a different name to "Breeze-Dark"
